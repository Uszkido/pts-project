⚙ The Process

From Purchase
to Protection

Four simple steps create an unbreakable chain of legal ownership that protects every Nigerian device owner.

01
Vendor Registers Device
A certified PTS vendor registers the IMEI, hardware DNA serials, device photos, and receipt at point of sale.
02
2FA Ownership Transfer
The buyer receives a 6-digit Handover Code to complete the cryptographic transfer — confirming legal ownership.
03
Certificate Issued
A Digital Device Ownership Certificate (DDOC) is generated — QR-linked to the national registry and downloadable as a PDF.
04
Lifetime Protection
If stolen, one report locks the device globally. Any vendor attempting to buy or service it is immediately alerted.
