# 🚀 PTS (Property Tracking System) — The "Evolutionary Echelons"

The PTS project is evolving from a simple registry into a National Hardware Intelligence Infrastructure. This roadmap outlines the strategic phases (Echelons) to upscale the system to its maximum potential.

## 🛡️ Echelon I: Foundation & Integrity (Current Focus)
Strengthening the core registry and establishing industry-standard trust.

### 📂 1. Device "DNA" Registry (Deep Hardware Traceability)
- **Concept**: Beyond just the IMEI, we now log unique serial numbers of internal components (Screen, Battery, Logic Board, Camera module).
- **Impact**: Combats "Chop Shops." If a stolen phone is dismantled, its "DNA" (parts) remains flagged. Any service center attempting to install a flagged part will trigger an immediate "Illegal Part Detected" alert.

### 🏛️ 2. Service Center "White-Label" System
- **Concept**: A dedicated portal for third-party repair shops. They use the PTS backend to manage their customers' repairs while PTS handles the "Verified Service" logic.
- **Impact**: Increases the resale value of devices serviced at "PTS-Certified" centers.

### 💰 3. Valuation Oracle & Currency Localization
- **Concept**: Dynamic price estimation based on device age, repair history, and risk score. Optimized for the local market (NGN ₦).
- **Impact**: Direct benchmark for trade-in values across the country.

---

## 📡 Echelon II: The "Guardian" Mesh & Connectivity
Expanding the network to detect devices even when they are offline or hidden.

### 📶 4. "Guardian" Mesh Network Protocol
- **Concept**: A decentralized recovery network. If a device is marked "STOLEN," other nearby devices with the PTS "Guardian" app can detect its Bluetooth/WiFi signature (even if the SIM is removed) and securely report its proximity to the owner and Police.
- **Impact**: Tracking without GPS or Cellular.

### 🌍 5. Interpol & Cross-Border Sync
- **Concept**: Real-time data bridge with international databases like GSMA and Interpol's Stolen Property Registry.
- **Impact**: An IMEI flagged in Nigeria becomes a "hot" item globally, preventing cross-border resale.

---

## ⚖️ Echelon III: The "Secondary Market" Sovereign
Making PTS the only safe way to buy and sell used devices.

### 🤝 6. "Secondary Market Escrow" (P2P Safe-Trade)
- **Concept**: An integrated escrow service for buying/selling. The buyer's funds are held by PTS until the device IMEI is cryptographically transferred using the 2FA Handover Code.
- **Impact**: Total elimination of scams in the used-phone market.

### 🛡️ 7. "Anti-Theft" Kill-Switch API
- **Concept**: A privileged API that can send a "Deadly Signal" to the device hardware (via MDM or Telco partnership) to brick the device permanently if it is reported as stolen.
- **Impact**: Makes stealing phones a zero-profit "suicide mission" for thieves.
